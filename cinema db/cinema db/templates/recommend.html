How the AI Works

01
TF-IDF Vectorisation
Every movie is converted to a weighted numeric vector based on genre, keywords, and director — rare terms score higher.
02
Cosine Similarity
We compute a similarity matrix across all films. A score of 1.0 means identical feature space; 0 means no overlap.
03
User Profile
Your favourites and watch history are aggregated. Similarity scores are summed and normalised to build a taste profile.
04
Mood Engine
Your answers to the Mood Finder map to weighted genre preferences, which re-rank the entire catalogue in real time.
